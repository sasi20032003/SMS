Top Attractions in Vijayawada

Kanaka Durga Temple

The Kanaka Durga Temple, situated on the Indrakeeladri hill, is one of the most revered temples in Vijayawada. It is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga and attracts thousands of pilgrims annually.

Prakasam Barrage

Prakasam Barrage is a major landmark across the Krishna River. It offers beautiful views of the river and is a popular spot for leisurely walks and evening outings.

Undavalli Caves

The Undavalli Caves are ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 4th-5th century. They are known for their impressive architecture and are an important historical site.

Mangalagiri

Mangalagiri, meaning 'Sacred Hill', is a famous pilgrimage site with historic temples and scenic views. It is known for the Mangalagiri Temple and its cultural significance.

Ambedkar Statue

The Buddha Statue on the River Krishna is one of the tallest statues in India. It stands as a symbol of peace and enlightenment and is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike.
